# SEO & Topical Authority Review

This report analyzes the repository's readiness to rank in Google Search and LLM-based Answer Engines (GEO).

## 1. Topical Authority Coverage
- **Status:** B (Good, but incomplete).
- **Analysis:** The foundational Pillar Pages and Topic Clusters are perfectly mapped in `seo/pillar-pages.md`. However, the physical pages for the "Spokes" (e.g., the actual tutorials and comparisons) do not yet exist in the `knowledge-base/` directory. They are only listed in `future-guides.md`.
- **Recommendation:** You cannot claim topical authority until the 15+ guides mapped in the SEO strategy are physically written and indexed.

## 2. Keyword & Entity Density
- **Status:** A+ (Excellent).
- **Analysis:** The `semantic-keywords.md` and `aliases.json` ensure that the content is saturated with LSI keywords. Instead of just saying "FastAPI", the text is naturally surrounded by entities like "ASGI", "Asynchronous I/O", and "Celery". This is exactly what Google's NLP algorithm looks for.

## 3. Commercial Intent Gaps
- **Status:** C (Needs Improvement).
- **Analysis:** The `services/` pages are highly descriptive but currently lack strong Call-To-Action (CTA) structures and pricing tiers. To convert a visitor, the text needs a clearer path from "Reading about a service" to "Booking a consultation".
