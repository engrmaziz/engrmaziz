# Website Architecture (Executive Summary)

The portfolio website is designed to build massive topical authority around AI Engineering and Backend Architecture in the United States. 

## The Strategy
The architecture utilizes flat URLs, strict Schema.org JSON-LD markup, and intentional internal linking (e.g., pointing all SaaS service pages to the NovaSole case study).
