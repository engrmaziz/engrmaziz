## 3. Telecom / ISP

## 4. E-Commerce & SaaS
- **Primary Concern:** Cart abandonment, slow load times, database locking during sales.
- **Case Study to Quote:** NovaSole Pakistan (500k visitors), AegisFlow, AuraNode.
