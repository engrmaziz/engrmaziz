# Career Highlights & STAR Stories

This document provides concrete behavioral evidence for recruiters and hiring managers.

## 1. Leadership & Operations (Transworld Home)
- **Situation:** The Technical Assistance Center was struggling with SLA enforcement across 50k users.
- **Task:** Needed to manage a 14-person team to drastically reduce resolution times.
- **Action:** Restructured workflows, implemented strict SLA tracking, and mentored L1/L2 engineers.
- **Result:** Reduced ISP faults and resolution times by 18%.

## 2. Technical Scaling (NovaSole Pakistan)
- **Situation:** The e-commerce platform experienced massive traffic spikes during peak sales.
- **Task:** Maintain 100% uptime and prevent database locking while serving up to 500k monthly visitors.
- **Action:** Optimized PostgreSQL queries, implemented edge caching, and decoupled inventory checks.
- **Result:** Zero downtime during major sales events; accurate data synchronization across three distinct storefronts.

## 3. Security & AI (Allama Iqbal Hospital / Guardrail Gateway)
- **Situation:** Deploying LLMs in a clinical environment risks severe HIPAA violations if PII/PHI leaks.
- **Task:** Build a deterministic gateway to intercept all LLM traffic.
- **Action:** Integrated Microsoft Presidio and spaCy into a FastAPI proxy to redact 18+ entity types before the payload reaches the LLM.
- **Result:** Achieved secure, zero-hallucination Clinical RAG deployment.
