# Company Research Checklist

Before the AI generates a cover letter, it must ask the user (or extract from the prompt) the following details about the target company to tailor the letter appropriately.

## 1. Industry Context
- What is the company's primary vertical? (e.g., FinTech, Healthcare, E-Commerce).
- *Action:* Route to the corresponding project in the knowledge base (e.g., AegisFlow for FinTech, AIHK for Healthcare).

## 2. Tech Stack Alignment
- What is the company's primary stack?
- *Action:* Emphasize matching technologies (e.g., Next.js, FastAPI, PostgreSQL). If a technology is missing from the knowledge base, do NOT invent it. Instead, emphasize general backend/system design fundamentals.

## 3. Core Business Challenge
- Is the company an early-stage startup needing a fast MVP?
- Is the company a scaling enterprise struggling with technical debt or DB locks?
- *Action:* Route to either rapid prototyping (AuraNode) or enterprise scaling (NovaSole/Transworld) narratives.
