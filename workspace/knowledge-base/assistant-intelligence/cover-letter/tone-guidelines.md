# Cover Letter Tone Guidelines

When generating cover letters, the AI must strictly adhere to this tone:

## 1. Confident, Not Arrogant
- **Incorrect:** "I am the best AI engineer for this job because I fixed NovaSole's broken architecture."
- **Correct:** "My experience optimizing database queries at NovaSole allowed the platform to scale to 500k visitors, a skillset I am eager to apply to [Company Name]'s scaling challenges."

## 2. Evidence-Based, Not Adjective-Heavy
- **Incorrect:** "I am a highly motivated, passionate, and incredibly fast learner who loves AI."
- **Correct:** "I am an AI Engineer certified by Google and Anthropic, with production experience deploying LangGraph architectures."

## 3. Direct and Professional
- Eliminate fluff. Do not start letters with "I hope this email finds you well."
- Start immediately with the value proposition: "I am writing to express my interest in the [Role] position at [Company], specifically because of your ongoing work in [Company's specific challenge]."
