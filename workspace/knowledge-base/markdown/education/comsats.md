# B.Sc. (Hons.) Electrical Engineering

## Overview
A comprehensive four-year undergraduate program in Electrical Engineering focusing on modern power systems, telecommunications, and IoT integration. This education provided the foundational first-principles thinking that underpins Musharraf Aziz's deterministic approach to software and AI architecture.

## Duration

## Degree & Major

**Equivalent Level:** EQF Level 6

## Final Year Project (FYP)
**Title:** LoRaWAN Smart Agriculture Decision Support System
**Role:** Lead Developer & Hardware Architect

### Project Details
Designed and implemented an end-to-end IoT infrastructure using LoRaWAN technology to monitor agricultural metrics (soil moisture, temperature, humidity) in real-time. The system featured a custom sensor pipeline communicating over long distances with minimal power consumption, feeding data into a central decision support system to optimize crop yields.

### Achievements
