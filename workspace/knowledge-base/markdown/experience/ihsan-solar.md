# Team Lead, Quality Assurance & NOC Development

## Overview
A critical operational leadership role combining hardware understanding (solar PV systems) with software monitoring. This role required building a Network Operations Center (NOC) from scratch to ensure maximum yield and uptime for deployed solar assets.

## Company
**Ihsan Solar Energy Pvt. Ltd.**

## Industry
Renewable Energy / Solar

## Employment Type
Full-Time

## Dates
**December 2022 – December 2023**

## Location
Raiwind, Pakistan

## Responsibilities
- Architect and develop the company's first centralized Network Operations Center (NOC).
- Lead a team of 4 engineers in monitoring and maintaining over 400+ kW of installed solar capacity.
- Design and implement strict Quality Assurance (QA) protocols for both hardware installation and software telemetry.
- Integrate inverter APIs and monitoring sensors into a unified dashboard for real-time fault detection.

## Technical Stack
- **Infrastructure:** Network Operations Center (NOC) architecture
- **Integration:** REST APIs (Inverter telemetry), Sensor data pipelines
- **Domain Knowledge:** Solar PV Math (SEI RE101), Electrical Engineering

## Architecture & Systems Worked On
1. **Unified NOC Dashboard:** A centralized monitoring system aggregating telemetry from diverse solar installations to detect yield drops, inverter faults, or grid anomalies in real-time.

## Business Problems & Solutions
- **Problem:** Solar assets were failing silently, leading to lost yield for clients and high truck-roll costs for the company to diagnose issues manually.
  - **Solution:** Built the NOC from zero, integrating real-time telemetry APIs to instantly alert the team of any performance degradation before the client noticed.
- **Problem:** Inconsistent installation quality leading to post-deployment operational faults.
  - **Solution:** Implemented rigorous QA protocols driven by data, reducing post-installation faults dramatically.

## Achievements & KPIs
- Reduced overall operational faults by **25%** within the first year.
- Successfully monitored and maintained **400+ kW** of active solar capacity.
- Awarded the **Productivity Leader Award** (July 2023) for operational excellence.

## Business Impact
The establishment of the NOC transitioned the company from reactive maintenance to proactive monitoring. This drastically reduced operational overhead, saved thousands in unnecessary dispatch costs, and significantly improved client satisfaction and solar yield.

## Recruiter Highlights
- Demonstrates strong cross-disciplinary engineering (Electrical/Solar + Software APIs).
- Proven experience building operations from "zero to one".
- Strong people leadership (managed a team of 4 engineers).
