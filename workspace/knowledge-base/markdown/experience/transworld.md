# Team Lead, Technical Assistance Center

## Overview
A high-pressure operational leadership role at a major Internet Service Provider (ISP). This position required managing large-scale network health, enforcing strict Service Level Agreements (SLAs), and leading a large technical support team to ensure uninterrupted connectivity for tens of thousands of users.

## Company
**Transworld Home (ISP)**

## Industry
Telecommunications / ISP

## Employment Type
Full-Time

## Dates
**March 2022 – November 2022**

## Location
Lahore, Pakistan

## Responsibilities
- Manage and mentor a 14-person Technical Assistance Center (TAC) team.
- Oversee the network health and technical support for a massive user base of 50,000+ active connections.
- Enforce strict SLAs for fault resolution and customer support escalations.
- Optimize internal ticketing and routing workflows to reduce average handling time (AHT) and fault resolution time.

## Technical Stack
- **Infrastructure:** ISP Networking architectures, CRM/Ticketing systems
- **Skills:** Incident Management, SLA Enforcement, Workflow Optimization, Team Leadership

## Business Problems & Solutions
- **Problem:** High fault resolution times were causing customer churn and SLA breaches.
  - **Solution:** Restructured the internal escalation workflows and implemented rigorous monitoring protocols, slashing fault resolution times by 18%.
- **Problem:** Managing a large, high-stress team required constant motivation and quality control.
  - **Solution:** Led by example with a competence-driven leadership style, fostering an environment that won multiple corporate awards for workplace commitment.

## Achievements & KPIs
- Achieved a stellar **98% SLA resolution rate** across the network.
- Maintained an impeccable **99.95% network uptime**.
- Reduced fault resolution time by **18%**.
- **Awards:** Employee of the Month (September 2022), Workplace Commitment Award (October 2022).

## Business Impact
Directly impacted customer retention and brand reputation by drastically reducing downtime and accelerating support resolutions. The operational optimizations saved significant man-hours and improved the overall efficiency of the 14-person TAC unit.

## Recruiter Highlights
- Extensive experience managing large teams (14 direct reports) early in career.
- Accustomed to extreme scale (50,000+ connections) and high-stakes uptime requirements (99.95%).
- Highly decorated (multiple awards within a 9-month span).
