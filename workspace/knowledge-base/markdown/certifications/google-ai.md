# Google AI Professional Certificate

## Overview
A rigorous professional program consisting of 7 distinct courses designed by Google engineers. The curriculum covers everything from foundational machine learning algorithms to advanced neural network design and deployment strategies on Google Cloud.

## Provider
**Google (via Coursera)**

## Issue Date
**April 2026** *(Anticipated/Completed based on timeline)*

## Skills & Learning Outcomes
- Neural Network Architecture (CNNs, RNNs).
- TensorFlow and Keras model building.
- Data pipeline engineering for ML models.
- Ethical AI and bias mitigation.

## Industry Value
Provides a verifiable, vendor-backed standard of competence in modern AI development, ensuring that foundational concepts (beyond just calling LLM APIs) are deeply understood.

## Career Impact
Solidified the theoretical foundation required to architect systems like AegisFlow, where custom PyTorch models were utilized for anomaly detection instead of relying solely on pre-trained LLMs.

## Recruiter Notes
This is a highly structured, multi-month program (not a weekend course) that demonstrates a serious commitment to the underlying mathematics and architecture of Artificial Intelligence.
