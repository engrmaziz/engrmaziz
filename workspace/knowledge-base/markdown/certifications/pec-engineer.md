# Registered Electrical Engineer

## Overview
Official recognition and registration as a professional engineer by the Pakistan Engineering Council (PEC), the statutory body regulating the engineering profession.

## Provider
**Pakistan Engineering Council (PEC)**

## Issue Date
**2021** (Active)

## Skills & Learning Outcomes
- Strict adherence to engineering ethics and safety standards.
- Formal recognition of B.Sc. (Hons.) in Electrical Engineering.
- Mandated continuous professional development (CPD).

## Industry Value
Provides legal and professional credibility. It guarantees that the individual has passed a rigorous academic curriculum recognized at a national level and adheres to professional engineering standards.

## Continuous Professional Development (CPD)
Actively maintains registration through ongoing CPD activities. In Sep-Oct 2025, completed 6 PEC CPD Webinars covering AI, Cloud Computing, Cybersecurity, Solar Energy, and Scientific Writing (Totaling 6 CPD points).
