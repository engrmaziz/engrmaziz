I'm **Musharraf Aziz** — a Professional Electrical Engineer turned AI Engineer, building at the intersection of **intelligence, automation, and scale**.

My background in industrial systems taught me one thing: complexity must be tamed with elegant solutions. I bring that same engineering discipline to every line of code and every model I deploy.

- 🧠 I architect **AI-native applications** — not wrappers, but systems with real intelligence baked in
- ⚡ I ship **production-grade SaaS** using Next.js 15, FastAPI, and cutting-edge LLMs

- 📍 Based in Pakistan · Open to global remote roles & high-impact collaborations
